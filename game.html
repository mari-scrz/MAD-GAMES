WELCOME TO OUR GAME

Yato was born in a small village in the mountains of Japan. From a young
age, he showed a great interest in spirits and mythological beings that
are part of Japanese culture. He spent his days exploring the forests near
his village in search of magical creatures and ancient legends. One day,
while exploring, Yato came across a strange living flame that seemed to
be in danger. Without hesitation, Yato approached and rescued the creature.
To his surprise, the flame followed him and became his faithful companion.
Since then, Yato and Emby have become an inseparable duo. Emby, the
living flame, has shown magical abilities and has helped Yato fight evil
creatures and solve more dangerous puzzles. After a long time exploring
the forests and mountains of his village, Yato decided it was time to
embark on a bigger adventure. Along with Emby, his faithful companion,
he has decided to explore the Japanese islands in search of new magical
creatures and ancient legends.
